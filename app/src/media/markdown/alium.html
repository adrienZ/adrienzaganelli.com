Alium Bikes

Context

This a school project for a real client who wanted to build his own bikes brand. We had to create it from scratch: website, social networks, seo, communication strategy...

Conception

The specificity of his brand is the customization of their bikes through a configurator in the purchase funnel. the website tone had to be neutral to better showcase the products

Technical

We made a custom Wordpress theme build with Timber and ACF PRO. For the e-commerce part, we used WooCommerce and severals plugins for technical features.

  • Cache
  • Paypal integration
  • Language translation (polylang)
  • Yoast SEO

The most interisting page to do was the bike configurator. To achieve this, we had to rewrite the plugin Visual products configurator.
